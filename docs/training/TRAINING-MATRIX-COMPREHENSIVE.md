## üîÑ TRAINING AUTOMATION LOGIC RULES

<div style="background: #E7F3FF; padding: 20px; border-left: 4px solid #145B8B; margin: 20px 0;">

### Auto-Assignment Triggers

**1. New Hire Triggers**
- Assign all "Day 1", "Week 1", "Month 1" training upon hire
- Role-specific training assigned based on job title
- Generate training schedule with due dates

**2. Role Change Triggers**
- Assign new role training within 1 week of promotion/transfer
- Maintain records of previous role training
- Flag competency assessments needed for new role

**3. Regulatory Update Triggers**
- HUD Code update ‚Üí Assign HUD training to all affected roles
- NEC code cycle ‚Üí Assign NEC training to electrical personnel
- CCR update ‚Üí Assign CCR training to compliance and quality roles

**4. Retraining Triggers**
- 30 days before retraining due date ‚Üí Auto-notification
- Overdue training ‚Üí Daily reminder + supervisor notification
- Certification expiration ‚Üí 60-day advance notification

**5. Incident-Based Triggers**
- NCR involving operator error ‚Üí Remedial training assignment
- NTA TPIA rejection ‚Üí Additional training evaluation
- Safety incident ‚Üí Safety retraining mandatory

**6. SOP/WI Update Triggers**
- Major SOP revision ‚Üí Training on changes for affected personnel
- New work instruction ‚Üí Station-specific training assignment
