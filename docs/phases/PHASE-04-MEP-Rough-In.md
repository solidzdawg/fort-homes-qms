# PHASE 04: MEP Rough-In

## Overview
Covers installation and inspection of mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems prior to insulation and drywall. Includes QC Hold #4 and TPIA Hold #3.

## Key Steps
- Electrical, plumbing, and HVAC rough-in
- Pressure/leak testing
- Inspection of penetrations and firestopping
- Internal QC Hold #4
- TPIA Hold #3
