# Fort and Home LLC QMS Manual — Modular Factory (Build‑in‑Place / Static Bay)

Version: 1.0
Effective Date: 2026-01-13

This Quality Management System (QMS) manual documents Fort and Home LLC's processes, controls and records for modular/off‑site manufacturing of build‑in‑place (static bay) units in compliance with applicable Colorado Department of Housing (CDOH) rules and third‑party inspection requirements.
