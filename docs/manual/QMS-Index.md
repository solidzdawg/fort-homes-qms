## USAGE GUIDE: HOW TO NAVIGATE THIS QMS

### For Management & Leadership

1. **Start Here:** [Quality Management System Manual](QMS-Manual-Main.md)
   - Understand organizational context, policy, and structure
   - Review quality objectives and KPIs
   - Assess organizational alignment

2. **Then Review:** [Professional Standards Guide](QMS-Layout-Framework.md)
   - Understand documentation architecture
   - Learn quality principles applied
   - Review implementation roadmap

3. **For Specific Phase:** Refer to relevant SOP (SOP-001, SOP-002, etc.)
   - Understand detailed procedures
   - Review quality acceptance criteria
   - Assess inspection protocols

### For Production Supervisors & Team Leaders

1. **Quick Start:** [Quick-Reference Card](QMS-Quick-Reference-Card.md)
   - Get 1-page overview of procedures
   - Find document naming conventions
   - Reference quality standards at a glance

2. **For Your Phase:** Refer to Phase-Specific SOP
   - Understand roles and responsibilities
   - Review materials & equipment requirements
   - Learn quality acceptance criteria

3. **During Execution:** Use corresponding Work Instruction
   - Step-by-step visual guide
   - Fastening patterns and measurements
   - Troubleshooting common issues

### For Quality Assurance & Inspectors

1. **Authority:** [Quality Management System Manual](QMS-Manual-Main.md)
   - Understand QMS authority and requirements
   - Review quality objectives and metrics
   - Learn nonconforming item process

2. **Procedure:** Refer to relevant SOP
   - Review hold-point specifications
   - Understand acceptance criteria
   - Learn inspection coordination requirements

3. **Documentation:** Use corresponding Inspection Form
   - Complete all required measurements
   - Document pass/fail decisions
   - Route nonconforming items per procedure

### For New Employees

1. **Orientation:** [Quick-Reference Card](QMS-Quick-Reference-Card.md)
   - Get overview of company quality system
   - Understand key terms and concepts
   - Learn where to find information

2. **Role Training:** Training & Competency Documents
   - Complete role-specific training
   - Verify competency assessment
   - Obtain sign-off from supervisor

3. **Job-Specific:** Work Instructions for your assignment
   - Follow step-by-step procedures
   - Ask supervisor about unclear sections
   - Report issues or suggestions for improvement
