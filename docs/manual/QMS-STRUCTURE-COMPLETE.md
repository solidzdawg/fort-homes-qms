## Quality Management System Workflows

### Document Control Workflow
SOP-001 establishes procedures for:
- Document creation and approval
- Version control and distribution
- Change management and updates
- Record retention (7 years minimum)
- Supersession and archival

### Training & Competency Workflow
SOP-002 establishes:
- New hire orientation (3 weeks)
- Job-specific training per phase
- Annual refresher requirements
- Competency assessment and sign-off
- Performance-based retraining

### Audit & Continuous Improvement
SOP-003 establishes:
- Quarterly internal audits
- Management review meetings (quarterly)
- Audit finding tracking and closure
- Corrective action follow-up
- Effectiveness verification

### Nonconformance Management
SOP-004 establishes:
- NCR creation and investigation
- Root cause analysis (5-Why method)
- CAPA development and implementation
- Effectiveness verification
- Trend analysis and prevention

### Production Planning
SOP-011 establishes:
- Production scheduling (15-day cycle target)
- Bay assignment per phase
- Material/resource coordination
- Progress tracking
- Bottleneck identification and resolution

### Hold Point Management
SOP-013 establishes:
- HP schedule and inspector coordination
- Module traveler tracking
- Pass/fail decision process
- Nonconformance response
- Release authorization
