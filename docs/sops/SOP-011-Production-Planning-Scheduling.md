## 1. Purpose

To establish procedures for module scheduling, bay assignment, and production timelines. This SOP ensures efficient production flow, optimal resource utilization, and on-time module delivery while maintaining quality standards.

---

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to:
- Module order reception and initial planning
- Production schedule development and communication
- Bay assignment and resource allocation
- Timeline tracking and adjustments
- Bottleneck identification and resolution

**Applies to:** Production Manager, Supervisors, Production Planning, all production personnel

## 5. Schedule Development & Planning

### 5.1 Initial Planning
- Receive customer orders with specifications
- Analyze bill of materials and production requirements
- Establish baseline production timeline
- Identify critical path items and dependencies

### 5.2 Master Schedule Creation
- Allocate bays and production stations
- Assign target completion dates per phase
- Schedule hold point inspections
- Reserve specialized labor and equipment

---

## 6. Bay Assignment & Resource Allocation

### 6.1 Bay Assignment Criteria
- Module size and complexity
- Equipment availability and setup requirements
- Labor skill requirements and availability
- Hold point inspection scheduling conflicts

### 6.2 Timeline Adjustments
- Monitor daily progress against schedule
- Communicate delays to Production Manager immediately
- Adjust dependent phase timelines as needed
- Document schedule changes and reasons

---

## 7. Production Timeline Communication

Weekly schedule updates distributed to all teams. Shift supervisors receive daily work assignments. Module travelers updated with target completion dates at each hold point.
