## 1. Purpose

To establish procedures for Third-Party Inspector Agency (TPIA) coordination, inspector scheduling, and inspection result documentation. This SOP ensures timely regulatory inspections, compliance with CDOH requirements, and proper documentation of third-party inspection findings throughout the production lifecycle.

---

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to:
- TPIA inspection scheduling and notification
- Third-party inspector arrival and module preparation
- Inspection result documentation and findings
- Corrective action tracking per inspection reports
- Regulatory compliance verification

**Applies to:** Quality Manager, Production Supervisor, Third-Party Inspectors, Plant Management

## 5. TPIA Inspection Scheduling

### 5.1 Inspector Notification
- Quality Manager notifies TPIA minimum 7 days before inspection window
- Provide module serial numbers and production status
- Specify inspection scope (initial, reinspection, final)
- Confirm inspector availability and scheduled date/time

### 5.2 Module Preparation
- Production Supervisor ensures modules meet inspection criteria
- All required documentation assembled in traveler
- Module cleaned and positioned per inspector requirements
- Access clearance and safety verification completed

---

## 6. Third-Party Inspection Process

### 6.1 Inspector Arrival & Documentation
- Quality Manager greets inspector and verifies credentials
- Provide inspection scope documentation and module information
- Inspector examines module per CDOH standards
- Quality Manager witnesses inspection and takes notes

### 6.2 Findings Documentation
- Inspector completes inspection report with findings
- Pass/fail/conditional determination documented
- Any deficiencies and required corrective actions listed
- Inspector and Quality Manager sign inspection report

---

## 7. Post-Inspection Follow-up

Upon receipt of inspection report, Quality Manager reviews findings with Production Supervisor. If corrective actions required, supervisor schedules rework and notifies inspector of rework completion date. Reinspection scheduled per CDOH requirements. Inspection results archived per document retention policy.
