## üîç 6. NONCONFORMANCE INVESTIGATION & ANALYSIS

### 6.1 Root Cause Analysis Process

**Within 5 days of NCR initiation:**


**ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS PROCESS**


**STEP 1: GATHER DATA**

‚Ä¢ Collect all relevant information
‚Ä¢ Review work instructions and actual practice
‚Ä¢ Interview involved personnel
‚Ä¢ Examine similar products/processes

**STEP 2: IDENTIFY CONTRIBUTING FACTORS**

‚Ä¢ Equipment failure?
‚Ä¢ Procedure not clear or followed?
‚Ä¢ Training/competency issue?
‚Ä¢ Environmental factor?
‚Ä¢ Supplier material issue?

**STEP 3: DETERMINE ROOT CAUSE**

‚Ä¢ Use "5 Why" analysis
‚Ä¢ Ask: Why did nonconformance occur?
‚Ä¢ Keep asking "Why" until true cause found
‚Ä¢ Avoid symptom-fixing

### 6.2 5 Why Analysis Example


**5 WHY ANALYSIS EXAMPLE**

üî¥ ISSUE: Floor deck assembled with ¬æ" gap between joists
Why 1: Installer didn't measure spacing
‚Üí Why didn't they measure?
Why 2: Tape measure not available at workstation
‚Üí Why not available?
Why 3: Tools not issued at shift start
‚Üí Why not procedure?
Why 4: No tool management system exists
‚Üí Why no system?
‚úÖ ROOT CAUSE: Lack of formal tool management procedure
