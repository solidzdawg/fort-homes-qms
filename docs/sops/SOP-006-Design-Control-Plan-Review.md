## 1. Purpose

To establish procedures for controlling the design and development of new modules and product variations, ensuring designs meet regulatory requirements (CDOH, HUD Code, IRC), customer needs, and company standards before release to production.

---

## 2. Scope

Applies to:
- New module designs
- Product variations and options
- Structural modifications
- Layout and floor plan changes
- Component substitutions

---

## 3. References & Standards

- CDOH HUD Code Section 3280
- IRC Chapter 5-6 (Building Code)
- ISO 9001:2015 Section 8.3 (Design Control)
- SOP-001 (Document Control)
- SOP-004 (Nonconformance)

---

---

## 5. Design Input

### 5.1 Requirements Documentation

**All designs must document:**
- Customer requirements (layout, features)
- Regulatory requirements (codes, standards)
- Structural requirements (loads, spans)
- Manufacturing requirements (available equipment)
- Cost constraints
- Timeline requirements

---

## 6. Design Development

### 6.1 Design Process

1. **Concept Development**
   - Preliminary sketches
   - Layout alternatives
   - Feasibility assessment

2. **Detailed Design**
   - Engineering calculations
   - Material specifications
   - Construction drawings
   - Assembly sequencing

3. **Design Review**
   - Engineering review
   - Production feasibility review
   - Compliance verification

---

## 7. Design Output & Documentation

**Deliverables:**
- Stamped engineering drawings (by PE)
- Bill of materials (BOM)
- Material specifications
- Assembly drawings
- Installation instructions
- Compliance certification

---

## 8. Design Verification & Validation

### 8.1 Verification Activities

- ✅ Calculations verified by independent PE
- ✅ Drawings reviewed for completeness
- ✅ Material specs confirmed available
- ✅ Manufacturing feasibility confirmed

### 8.2 Validation Activities

- ✅ First module built per design
- ✅ Structural testing (if new design)
- ✅ Code compliance verification
- ✅ Customer acceptance

---

## 9. Regulatory Approval

**Before production release:**
- Obtain CDOH approval (if required)
- Obtain PE stamp on drawings
- File compliance certification
- Document approval dates

---

## 10. Design Changes

- Follow SOP-005 (Change Management)
- Notify regulatory authority (if applicable)
- Verify continued compliance
- Update all documentation

---

## 11. Design Records

Maintain:
- Design requirements document
- Design calculations
- Engineering drawings
- Approval records
- Regulatory certifications
- Design change history
