### A6. RESNET GRADE I INSTALLATION QUALITY

**RESNET Grading System:**
- **Grade I**: Insulation fills cavity completely, no gaps/voids/compression >2%, split around penetrations
- **Grade II**: Minor gaps/voids (2-5% of area), some compression
- **Grade III**: Significant gaps/voids (>5%), major compression, missing insulation

| Item | Criteria | Result (P/F) | Comments |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| A6.1 | No gaps or voids >2% of insulated area | [ ] | Estimated void %: _____ |
| A6.2 | No compression >2% of insulated area | [ ] | Estimated compression %: _____ |
| A6.3 | Insulation fills entire cavity depth (no air space behind) | [ ] | Verified |
| A6.4 | Insulation split or fitted around penetrations (wires, pipes, boxes) | [ ] | Verified |
| A6.5 | No "c-wraps" or insufficient splits | [ ] | Verified |
| A6.6 | Overall installation quality: Grade I | [ ] | **Grade: I / II / III** |

**If Grade II or III, corrective action required before drywall installation.**
