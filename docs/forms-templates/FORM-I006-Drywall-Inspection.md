### 5. FINISH QUALITY

**Finish Levels per ASTM C840:**
- **Level 3**: Tool marks and ridges acceptable, suitable for heavy texture
- **Level 4**: Smooth surface, ready for flat paint or light texture (FORT HOMES STANDARD)
- **Level 5**: Highest quality, glass-smooth, for critical lighting or gloss paint

| Item | Criteria | Result (P/F) | Inspection Method / Comments |
