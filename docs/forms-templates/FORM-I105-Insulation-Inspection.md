## Quality Acceptance Criteria

**PASS Criteria (all must be met):**
- [ ] All visual inspection items = PASS
- [ ] 100% cavity coverage (≥99%)
- [ ] Correct R-values for walls, floor, roof
- [ ] Proper air sealing throughout
- [ ] No compression damage
- [ ] No gaps >1/2" in cavities

**FAIL Criteria (any one causes rejection):**
- [ ] Coverage <99%
- [ ] Incorrect R-value materials
- [ ] Gaps >1/2" in cavities
- [ ] Compressions reducing R-value
- [ ] Missing air sealing
- [ ] Improper facing orientation
