## Quality Acceptance Criteria

**PASS Criteria (all must be met):**
- [ ] All trim installation items = PASS
- [ ] All fastening and adhesive items = PASS
- [ ] All material and finish items = PASS
- [ ] All joints tight (<1/16" gap)
- [ ] All caulked joints smooth and filled
- [ ] Professional appearance, no defects

**FAIL Criteria (any one causes rejection):**
- [ ] Gaps >1/16" in joints or trim
- [ ] Misalignment >1/8" from plumb/level
- [ ] Missing or improper fasteners (>2%)
- [ ] Cracks, splits, or damage visible
- [ ] Finish not uniform or professional
- [ ] Debris or contamination present
