| 6.6 | Gable vent NFA provided (if gable vents used) | [ ] | NFA: _____ sq in |
| 6.7 | Total NFA meets or exceeds requirement | [ ] | Total NFA: _____ ≥ Required: _____ |
| 6.8 | Balanced ventilation (intake ≈ exhaust if soffit/ridge combo) | [ ] | Intake: _____ Exhaust: _____ |
| 6.9 | Ventilation baffles installed at soffit (maintain airflow) | [ ] | Installed: Y / N |

**IRC R806 Ventilation Requirements:**
- Minimum 1:150 ratio (attic floor sq ft : net free vent area sq in)
- OR 1:300 ratio if balanced ventilation (50% intake low, 50% exhaust high)

**Example Calculation:**
- Attic area: 400 sq ft
- Required NFA: 400 ÷ 150 = 2.67 sq in minimum (unbalanced)
- OR 400 ÷ 300 = 1.33 sq in minimum (balanced)
