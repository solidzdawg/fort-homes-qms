# SOP-002: Floor Framing (Wood Joists & Subfloor)

**Purpose:** Ensure floor framing is installed per plans, meets IRC/ICC NTA requirements, and is ready for TPIA inspection.

---

## 1. Scope
- Applies to all wood-based floor framing systems in FH modular factory.
- Excludes metal floor systems.

## 2. Responsible Parties
| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| Floor Crew Lead | Execute work, sign off internal QC |
| FH QC Inspector | Perform internal QC hold |
| ICC NTA Inspector | Perform TPIA hold |

## 3. Prerequisites
- Material verification (QC Hold #1 complete)
- Joist layout marked per plans
- All fasteners, hangers, adhesives on hand

## 4. Work Instructions
- See WI-002A, WI-002B, WI-002C

## 5. Hold Points
- QC Hold #2: Pre-TPIA Floor Framing (internal)
- TPIA Hold #1: Floor Framing Complete (external)

## 6. Acceptance Criteria
| Item | Criteria | Tolerance |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Joist Spacing | Per plan | ±1/8" |
| Joist Bearing | Full bearing on plates | 90%+ |
| Fastener Schedule | Per plan | 100% compliance |
| Subfloor Gaps | 1/8" at edges | ±1/16" |
| Adhesive Coverage | Continuous bead | 100% |
| Moisture Content | ≤19% | IRC R502.1 |

## 7. Records & Forms
- FORM-I001: Floor Framing Inspection Checklist
- QC Hold #2 sign-off
- TPIA Hold #1 sign-off

## 8. Nonconformance Handling
- Mark failed items with red tag, document on NCR form
- Rework as directed, re-inspect before proceeding
