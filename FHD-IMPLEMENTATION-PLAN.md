# FHD Field QMS - Implementation Action Plan
## Fort and Homes Development (FHD) Quality Management System for Field Operations

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Created:** Research Phase Complete  
**Status:** Ready for Execution  
**Target Completion:** 8-12 weeks

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Fort and Homes Development (FHD) will implement a comprehensive, hybrid Quality Management System that:
1. Extends Fort and Homes LLC factory QMS to field operations
2. Complies fully with HUD Code (24 CFR 3280/3282) requirements
3. Incorporates industry best practices for modular home field assembly
4. Implements advanced holdpoint strategy for quality assurance
5. Integrates with IPIA (Independent Production Inspection Agency) oversight
6. Provides transparent customer experience through modern technology

### 1.2 Proposed FHD Organizational Structure

```
FHD Operations Director
├── Field Operations Manager (supervises field supervisors)
│   ├── Field Supervisor - Site 1 (manages installation crew 1)
│   ├── Field Supervisor - Site 2 (manages installation crew 2)
│   └── Field Supervisor - Site 3 (manages installation crew 3)
├── Quality Assurance Manager (audits field work, IPIA liaison)
│   ├── QA Inspector 1 (on-site quality verification)
│   ├── QA Inspector 2 (documentation, compliance)
│   └── Licensed Trade Coordinator (electrician/plumber/gas tech oversight)
├── Customer Experience Manager (communications, warranty)
│   └── Installation Scheduler (timeline management, coordination)
└── Compliance Officer (regulatory, documentation, legal)
```

**Staffing:** Start with 2-4 core staff + contracted field crews per site

### 1.3 Key Roles & Responsibilities

#### Field Supervisor (FI-001 through FI-007 field work)
- Responsible for day-to-day installation execution
- Ensures work quality and safety
- Conducts pre-inspection before QA verification
- Manages field team and subcontractors
- Documents work completion per checklists

#### QA Inspector (verification & validation)
- Independent verification of work quality
- Holdpoint inspection sign-off
- IPIA coordination and communication
- Deficiency documentation and corrective action tracking
- Final site inspection report preparation

#### Licensed Trade Coordinator
- Manages relationships with licensed electricians, plumbers, gas technicians
- Verifies certifications and insurance
- Ensures licensed work performed per specifications
- Collects and files trade certifications for final record

#### Compliance Officer
- Regulatory framework oversight
- Documentation completeness verification
- IPIA and Colorado agency liaison
- Deficiency tracking and trending analysis

### WEEK 3-5: CORE FIELD INSTALLATION SOPs

#### **Week 3: Site Prep & Module Placement**

**FI-001: Site Preparation & Foundation Verification (2 days to develop)**
- Foundation design validation requirements
- Ground leveling specifications (±1/4" HUD standard)
- Utility pre-staging and marking procedures
- Site safety setup procedures
- Holdpoints: Foundation certification, utility clearance, site access

**FI-003: Module Placement & Leveling (2 days)**
- Module positioning per design
- Leveling procedures and tolerance specifications
- Support pier installation and verification
- Module-to-pier connection procedures
- Holdpoints: Leveling certification, pier inspection, support verification

**Work Instructions & Forms (2 days):**
- WI-FI-001: Site Preparation (with diagrams showing foundation, utility layout)
- WI-FI-003: Module Leveling (with visual step-by-step instructions)
- FORM-FI-001: Site Preparation Inspection Checklist
- FORM-FI-003: Module Leveling Verification Form

#### **Week 4: Module Delivery & Assembly**

**FI-002: Module Transport & Delivery Coordination (2 days)**
- Transport routing and logistics planning
- Module condition inspection procedures
- Damage assessment and documentation
- Temporary module support procedures
- Holdpoints: Pre-transport certification, route clearance, post-delivery condition

**FI-004: Module Connection & Sealing (2 days)**
- Inter-module connection assembly procedures
- Weather-resistant sealing specifications
- Structural connection verification
- Temporary support removal procedures
- Holdpoints: Connection tightness, sealant curing verification, structural integrity

**Work Instructions & Forms (2 days):**
- WI-FI-002: Module Transport & Delivery
- WI-FI-004: Module Connection Assembly
- FORM-FI-002: Module Condition & Delivery Inspection
- FORM-FI-004: Connection Assembly & Sealing Checklist

#### **Week 5: Utility Connections**

**FI-005: Utility Final Connections (3 days to develop)**
- Water supply connection procedures (per 24 CFR 3280.603)
- Sewer/waste connection installation
- Gas piping final connection (licensed technician requirement)
- Electrical final hookup procedures
- Appliance connections
- System testing and certification procedures
- Holdpoints: Plumber inspection, electrician certification, gas technician certification, all systems tested

**Work Instructions & Forms (2 days):**
- WI-FI-005A: Water & Sewer Connections (detailed plumbing diagram)
- WI-FI-005B: Electrical Final Connections (wiring and testing procedures)
- WI-FI-005C: Gas Piping Connections (licensed technician requirements)
- FORM-FI-005: Utility System Testing & Certification Checklist

### WEEK 6-7: FINAL OPERATIONS & FIELD QMS PROCEDURES

#### **Week 6: Finishing & Final Inspection**

**FI-006: Site Finishing (1.5 days)**
- Exterior finishing procedures (siding, roofing, trim)
- Deck/ramp installation (if applicable)
- Grading and drainage finalization
- Site cleanup procedures
- Holdpoints: All exterior penetrations sealed, grading verified

**FI-007: Final Inspection & Customer Handoff (1.5 days)**
- IPIA on-site inspection coordination procedures
- Final site inspection report requirements (per 24 CFR 3282.605)
- Customer walkthrough procedures
- Warranty document transfer
- Record filing and project closeout
- Holdpoints: IPIA inspection passed, all deficiencies corrected, customer satisfied

**Work Instructions & Forms (1 day):**
- WI-FI-006: Site Finishing Procedures
- WI-FI-007: Final Inspection Coordination (IPIA interface)
- FORM-FI-006: Site Finishing Inspection Checklist
- FORM-FI-007: Final Customer Walkthrough & Handoff

#### **Week 7: Field QMS Procedures (8 core SOPs)**

**FHD-OP-001: Document Control - Field** (0.5 day)
- Version control for field installation instructions
- Distribution and retrieval of field documents
- Obsolete document removal procedures
- Records retention timeline

**FHD-OP-002: Field Team Training** (0.5 day)
- Competency requirements for field supervisors
- New team member training curriculum
- Annual training and refresher requirements
- Competency assessment procedures

**FHD-OP-003: Field Quality Audits** (0.5 day)
- Unannounced on-site quality inspections
- Audit checklist and scoring
- Frequency and scheduling
- Audit findings and corrective actions

**FHD-OP-004: Field CAPA (Corrective Action)** (0.5 day)
- Handling deficiencies discovered during inspections
- Root cause analysis procedures
- Corrective action implementation
- Effectiveness verification

**FHD-OP-005: Change Control - Field** (0.5 day)
- Managing design changes during on-site completion
- Change authorization and approval
- Documentation and impact assessment
- Customer communication procedures

**FHD-OP-006: Licensed Trade Coordination** (0.5 day)
- Managing electricians, plumbers, gas technicians
- Certification and insurance verification
- Licensed work scope definition
- Trade-specific quality requirements
- Records management

**FHD-OP-007: IPIA Coordination** (0.5 day)
- Interface procedures with independent inspection agency
- Holdpoint inspection scheduling and notification
- Final on-site inspection coordination
- Documentation and record transfer

**FHD-OP-008: Installation Metrics & Reporting** (1 day)
- KPI definitions and tracking
- Monthly installation reports
- Quality metrics trending
- Performance dashboards and reporting
- Continuous improvement data analysis

**Week 7 Deliverables:**
